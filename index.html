Salient Venture Sales Analysis

This Project involves analyzing the sales data of Salient Venture for new insights generation that supports board decision making. The objective was to identify key sales trends and opportunities for growth and verify the alignment between the digital storefront strategies and actual consumer behavior.

Through detailed data analysis, we were able to uncover previously hidden patterns in customer purchasing behavior, which informed strategic decisions and led to a 15% increase in sales. We also identified tight margins and recommended to management to defend margins not just revenue.

Our recommendations also helped managed reduce single-market concentration risk, tackle the largest cost drivers behind expenses and protect top products and key accounts. This analysis was performed using Microsoft Excel.

Retail and Omnichannel Sales Analysis

Wexford & Rowan was experiencing a Q3 visibility gap. Despite strong sales momentum, leadership could not clearly see which categories, States, and customer segments drove performance. This created a risk that Q4 stock allocation, marketing budgets, and staffing decisions may be misaligned. The result of the analysis was we were able to clean the Q3 sales dataset, analyse demand patterns across categories, States, time slots, age groups, and payment methods, and deliver a dynamic Excel dashboard for the commercial leadership team.
